Our Services

Comprehensive flooring solutions tailored to your needs.

Hardwood Flooring

Nothing beats the warmth and character of genuine hardwood. We offer expert installation of solid and engineered wood floors. We also specialize in sanding and refinishing existing hardwood to bring it back to its original glory.

  • Solid & Engineered Wood Installation
  • Dust-Free Sanding & Refinishing
  • Staining & Custom Finishes
  • Repair & Restoration

Tile & Stone

From elegant ceramic to durable porcelain and natural stone, our title services cover every room in your house. Kitchen backsplashes, bathroom showers, and custom entryways are just the beginning.

  • Ceramic, Porcelain & Natural Stone
  • Custom Showers & Backsplashes
  • Heated Tile Floors
  • Grout Restoration

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)

Get the look of wood or stone with the durability and waterproof properties of vinyl. LVP is an excellent choice for basements, kitchens, and busy households with pets and kids.

  • Waterproof Flooring Solutions
  • Floating & Glue-Down Installation
  • Durable & Scratch Resistant
  • Wide Variety of Styles

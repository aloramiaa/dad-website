About Duffus Flooring

Dedicated to Quality, Integrity, and Craftsmanship.

Our Story

Founded on the principles of hard work and dedication, Duffus Flooring has been serving the community with exceptional flooring solutions for over a decade. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted name in the industry, known for our attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction.

We believe that your floors are the foundation of your home. They set the tone for your entire living space. That's why we take the time to understand your vision and bring it to life with precision and care.

Our Mission

"To provide superior flooring installations and repairs with unwavering honesty and craftsmanship, creating spaces that our customers love to come home to."
